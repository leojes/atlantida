Structured Cabling - services

Structured cabling performs the function of a digital information backbone that ties together all the modern communication environments together. GCN conceptualizes and deploys dynamic, cost-efficient network communication solutions that are built to satisfy the needs of your burgeoning business. We offer over 100 manufacturing pipelines, allowing us to provide a comprehensive set of solutions of any size, alongside inspection and technical support, making your business seamlessly integrated and working non-stop. No matter the size or complexity of the task we're handed, GCN can manage all your multimedia, video, voice and data needs.

The GCN Advantage

Working with NCS provides you and your business with several benefits:

  • Supervision of the entire cabling deployment project from one source, starting with design and finishing with execution.
  • Comprehensive turnkey ecosystem with minimal time-to-deploy and an innate solution: elevation, design and rack design..
  • Standards-based implementation according to specifications
  • Properly documented and labeled cabling layout, facilitating immediate change, easy management, fast diagnostics and minimal system downtime.

Standards Compliance

GCN exceeds or meets the following national standards regarding uniform documentation, testing, installation and labeling for telecom cabling within commercial buildings, such as EN 50173, ISO 11801, TIA-606, TIA-568 and TIA-942
These standards guarantee that NCS will deliver a uniform environment that can be used straight away thanks to its compatibility with a multitude of vendors.
