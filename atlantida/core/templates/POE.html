Power over Ethernet (PoE) Lighting

Lighting systems powered through a network provide you with a smart system that fully leverages Internet of Things (IoT) technology. From HVAC to sensors and many other devices, you can create a custom lighting solution for your enterprise environment. Thanks to PoE-enabled networks, you can gather data, inspire and interact with the staff and provide personalization options for everyone visiting or working on the premises. Ultimately, PoE provides comfort, safety and productivity.

Applications

Healthcare

More than one study has shown that lighting affects physical and mental health of individuals. Thanks to PoE architecture, healthcare providers can monitor the well-being of patients and adjust the micro-climate, which ultimately has a net positive effect on patients. From day to night, the patients can be provided with just the right level and type of lighting so they recover faster. For precise tasks, such as delicate surgery, PoE also has a solution, adjusting as needed to provide better visibility and a brighter environment with a more natural light.

Gunshot detection

School faculty can now have a reliable way to detect gunshots, instantly alerting everyone present and the law enforcement where they happened. This can provide invaluable, real-time insight into shooting incidents and other events happening on school grounds. Sensors deployed throughout the building can trigger to notify first responders and occupants as of the whereabouts of the shooter or any other danger.

Enhanced learning environment

School experience is intimately linked to glaring and flickering fluorescent lights. These lights, ubiquitous in schools, do not foster a pleasant learning environment and can actually be detriment to learning. In such an environment, the brain refuses to focus and wanders off. In fact, studies have shown brightness levels correlate well with students grades. Better yet, IoT systems allow for a finer humidity, temperature and oxygen levels inside the premises.

Environments You Can Monitor and Adjust

  • Temperature
  • Humidity.
  • Light output
  • Color temperature
  • Circadian rhythm
  • CO2 detection
  • Gunshot detection
  • Ambient light (daylight harvesting)

Get Real-Time Insights

Once deployed, a PoE network provides you with a user-friendly and smooth web-centered interface through which you can monitor and control connected devices. The real-time aggregated data can provide you with insights about anything from occupancy to activity patterns and temperature. Thanks to the LED lighting serving as a backbone in the IP network, you are given an enhanced capability for decision-making when it comes to lighting needs of occupants. Eco-friendly materials give PoE lighting systems a longer operating life, reduced energy wastage and improved dimmability with more colors.
